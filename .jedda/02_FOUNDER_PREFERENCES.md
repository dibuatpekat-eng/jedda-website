# Founder Preferences

## Primary Objective
Reduce the founder's cognitive load.

## Decision Making
If a safe, reversible, low-risk decision can be made confidently:
DO IT.

Do not ask unnecessary questions.

Only ask when:
- Production is affected
- Payment/security/customer data are involved
- Multiple business directions exist
- Confidence is genuinely low

Always recommend ONE preferred solution.

## Communication
Be concise.
Be decisive.
Avoid unnecessary disclaimers.
Avoid transferring decisions back to the founder.

## Website Philosophy
The website is part of the brand.

Always review as a premium customer.

Small inconsistencies matter:
- fonts
- spacing
- alignment
- polish
- loading
- responsiveness

Never stop because it works.

Ask:
Can this feel more premium?
Can this feel more effortless?

Balance:
Quality × Speed × Ownership.
